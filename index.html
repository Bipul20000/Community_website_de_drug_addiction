About Parivartan

Parivartan is a dedicated organization committed to helping children overcome addiction and rebuild their lives. Through our comprehensive outreach programs, we provide support, education, and rehabilitation to young individuals struggling with substance abuse.

Our team of experienced social workers, counselors, and volunteers work tirelessly to create a nurturing environment where children can heal, learn, and grow. We believe in the power of transformation and the potential within every child to overcome their challenges.

"Every child deserves a chance to rewrite their story and create a brighter future."

Our 3-Month Program Flow

Assessment & Intake

Initial evaluation and personalized treatment plan

Detoxification

Medical support for safe withdrawal from substances

Rehabilitation

Intensive therapy and skill-building sessions

Education & Life Skills

Academic support and practical life skills training

Family Involvement

Family therapy and support groups

Aftercare Planning

Developing a long-term recovery strategy
